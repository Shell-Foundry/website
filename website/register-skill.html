Frequently Asked Questions

How long does approval take?

Usually within 24 hours. We review submissions to prevent spam and ensure quality.

When do I get paid?

Instantly! Every time a user pays for your skill, 90% goes directly to your wallet. No delays, no minimums.

Can I change the price later?

Yes! You can update your skill's price anytime after registration. Just contact us.

What if my skill is free?

Set the price to 0. ClawPay will still verify access but won't charge users.
